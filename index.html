What We Build

Custom Barndominiums

Combining high-grade steel frames with luxury interiors. Our East Texas barndominiums offer energy-efficient, open-concept living at a fraction of the cost of traditional builds.

Steel Workshops

Expertly engineered metal workshops and hobby shops. Built to withstand Texas winds, our steel structures provide secure, clear-span space for any project.

Agricultural Barns

Heavy-duty metal farm buildings designed for livestock, hay storage, and equipment. Our rust-resistant steel barns are the backbone of East Texas agriculture.

Detached Garages

Premium metal garage construction featuring reinforced steel siding. Ideal for vehicle storage, home gyms, or man-caves with custom roll-up door options.

Commercial Steel

Scalable industrial metal buildings for retail, office space, or manufacturing. Rapid turn-key construction services to get your business up and running faster.

RV & Boat Covers

Protect your investments with custom steel RV carports and boat covers. Engineered for maximum clearance and superior protection against sun and hail damage.
